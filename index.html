The Akida Film Festival, is an international celebration of creativity, featuring
exceptional short films.

- Over 50 short films

- Produced in 14 different Countries

- Hosted by Union Cinema City in Seville

- Castings are followed by Q&A sessions

Best Thriller

Best Documentary

Best Cinematography

Best Actress

Best Actor

Best Director

Most Unique and Original Story
